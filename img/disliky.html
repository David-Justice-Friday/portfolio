• A video of a child drinking a suspicious concoction from her mother has been doing the rounds on social media
  • The hilarious clip that went viral showed the child's mother preparing spinach water for the little girl who wanted juice
  • The child's reaction was priceless, leaving many social media users amused by the African parent's antics in the video

Children will always try their luck to get what they want, even when they're not supposed to. One slick mother tricked her sick little one into drinking spinach water instead of juice.

A video posted by TikTok user @cal.mi_ck showed a woman collecting a jug of water from a pot of boiled spinach and decanting it into a juice box before giving it to her child to drink.

The curious child proceeded to drink the concoction and reacted shockingly to the taste as she continued to drink reluctantly. Unsatisfied with the drink, the child soon pushed the juice box away with a disgusted look.

The funny video was captioned:

"When your mum is your biological mother."

According to Healthline, spinach juice is linked to many impressive health benefits. It is a nutritional powerhouse, rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

This smart mother was helping her little one rather than just playing a sneaky prank on her, LOL.

Mum pranks son into thinking he's drinking alcohol

In another article, a video was posted on Facebook showing the lad holding a green bottle of liquid with the label removed, to which his mother asked what he was having and why he took off the branding.

The boy showed his mum the paper he had left on the floor while explaining that what he was having tasted sour.

The mum then pranked the boy into thinking he had been sipping on some liquor, to which the child completely lost it.
